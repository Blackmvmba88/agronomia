## üé¨ Production Tips

### Lighting
- Natural daylight or softbox lights
- Avoid harsh shadows
- White balance correction in post

### Audio
- Royalty-free music (e.g., from YouTube Audio Library)
- Keep volume low, not distracting
- Alternative: No audio (silent video with text)

### Editing Software
**Free Options:**
- DaVinci Resolve (professional features)
- OpenShot (simple, cross-platform)
- Kdenlive (Linux-friendly)

**Paid Options:**
- Adobe Premiere Pro
- Final Cut Pro (Mac)
- Camtasia (easy screen recording)

### Screen Recording
- **OBS Studio** (free, powerful)
- **QuickTime** (Mac built-in)
- **Windows Game Bar** (Windows built-in)

Settings:
- 1920x1080 resolution
- 30-60 fps
- Hardware encoding if available

## üé® Visual Style Guidelines

### Color Palette
- Primary: Green (#4CAF50) - agriculture
- Secondary: Blue (#2196F3) - technology
- Accent: Orange (#FF9800) - alerts/energy
- Background: White/Light gray for clarity

### Typography
- **Title Font**: Bold, sans-serif (e.g., Montserrat)
- **Body Font**: Clean, readable (e.g., Open Sans)
- **Size**: Large enough to read on mobile

### Transitions
- Keep simple: Fade, dissolve, cut
- Avoid flashy effects
- Maintain 1-2 second transition timing

---

## üìù Text Overlay Best Practices

### Keep It Short
- Maximum 5-7 words per overlay
- Use action words
- Highlight key features

### Positioning
- Lower third is standard
- Avoid corners (safe zones)
- Ensure readability against background

### Animation
- Fade in/out (0.5s duration)
- Or simple slide animation
- Consistent timing across all text
